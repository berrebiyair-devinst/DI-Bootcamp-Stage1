About Ilan Ramon

Ilan Ramon was an Israeli Air Force pilot and astronaut. He was the first Israeli astronaut and a payload specialist on the Space Shuttle Columbia mission STS-107. Tragically, he lost his life along with the rest of the crew during the reentry of the shuttle on February 1, 2003.

Timeline of Ilan Ramon's Life:

  • 1954 : Born in Ramat Gan, Israel.
  • 1981 : Participated in Operation Opera as the youngest pilot.
  • 1997 : Selected by NASA as a payload specialist.
  • 2003: Flight aboard Columbia and tragic death during atmospheric reentry on February 1st.
